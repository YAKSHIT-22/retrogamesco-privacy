Privacy Policy — Retro Games Co

Last updated: 2026-07-03. This single policy covers all games in the Retro Games Co.

We build small retro games. We do not require you to create an account or sign in, and we do not knowingly collect personally identifying information such as your name, email, or contact list.

Information collected automatically

Our games use the following third-party services, which may collect data as described in their own policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — may collect device identifiers and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config, Performance Monitoring) — collects anonymized usage events (e.g. level started/completed, session start), crash diagnostics, device model, OS version, and an app-instance identifier. Used only to improve the games.
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases, in games that offer them) — processes purchases. We receive confirmation of a purchase but not your payment card details.

We do not sell your data. We do not build advertising profiles beyond what AdMob/Firebase provide for ad delivery and analytics.

Children

Our games are directed to a general audience and are not targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your choices

  • You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • You can request deletion of analytics data associated with your app instance by contacting us.
