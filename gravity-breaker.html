Privacy Policy — Gravity Breaker

Last updated: 2026-07-18 · Package com.retrogamesco.gravitybreaker

Gravity Breaker does not require an account or sign-in. We do not knowingly collect personally identifying information.

Information collected automatically

This game uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — may collect device identifiers and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads.
  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config, Performance Monitoring) — anonymized usage events, crash diagnostics, device model, OS version, an app-instance identifier. Used only to improve the game.
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases) — processes purchases. We receive purchase confirmation, not your payment card details.

We do not sell your data.

Children

This game is directed to a general audience and is not targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your choices

  • You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • You can request deletion of analytics data associated with your app instance by contacting us.
